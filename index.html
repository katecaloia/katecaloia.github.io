Homepage Redesign

One of the most challenging redesign's I have worked on to date was the WebstaurantStore homepage. While many users first point of contact with WebstaurantStore isn't necessarily the homepage, it's still an important part of our traffic flow. There were a lot of stakeholders involved in this project and one of our biggest selling points was tailoring the homepage experience to the customer. With recommended products, a status bar for orders, and a category grid with top grossing categories so our customers can find what they need and fast. I reworked all the homepage CSS and markup.

Food Service Resources

Food Service Resources is the home of WebstaurantStore's collective written resources used to inform and educate customers on potential purchases. When this project started our resource center had just begun to expand, launching into infographics, how to's, and in-depth articles. From a customer stand-point this information provides them with expert knowledge from our content team while also builds and backs our reputation as the largest online restaurant equipment supplier. This project required many sketches, a complete markup overhaul, and lots of work to make this flexbox beauty IE foolproof.

Product Page Admin & Design

Of all the projects I've worked on at WebstaurantStore, the new product page admin is perhaps my favorite. The essential part of this project was delivering a new product page experience that showcased the extensive in-house product descriptions we provide with photography to compliment. The biggest perk of this was not just a brand new product page but a shiny new admin for our content team. I built an admin tool that allowed our team to mix and match templates, as well as easily reference and pull product features for the 100K+ items we carry. Creating tools that improve everyday life for various departments at WebstaurantStore is the heart of why I love design.
